Case study format

Designed to be easy to scan in three minutes.

Each case follows a clear structure inspired by Big Tech job signals and reference portfolios: quick context, visual evidence, decisions, validation, systems, outcome, and reflection.

  1. What I did

    Role, timeline, team, scope, and exact design contribution.

  2. In a nutshell

    One sentence problem, one sentence solution, one clear outcome.

  3. Final design first

    Key screens, flow, prototype, or systems artifact before long explanation.

  4. Design decisions

    Research inputs, tradeoffs, constraints, collaboration, and iteration.

  5. Outcome

    Metrics, launch status, qualitative signals, and honest next steps.
