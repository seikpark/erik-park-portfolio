Flagship case

Butler

Redesigned a fintech landing and home experience to improve clarity, trust, and repeat usage.

Role Product Designer / Product Owner
Timeline 2023
Scope Landing, home, product clarity, retention loop
Outcome +40% retention

Problem

The product needed a clearer first-use and repeat-use experience for financial-service customers.

Contribution

I redesigned the landing and home flow, clarified value messaging, and aligned product decisions with delivery constraints.

Design focus

  • Reduce ambiguity in the entry experience.
  • Make product value easier to scan and trust.
  • Connect UX decisions to retention and delivery timing.
