Service design case

SM Meta-passport

Designed membership flows and interface patterns for a global entertainment fan ecosystem.

Role Product Designer / Design Manager
Timeline 2022
Scope Membership flows, IA, global fan ecosystem
Status Public-safe visuals

Problem

Fans needed a membership experience that could support identity, access, and participation across a global entertainment service.

Contribution

I designed core user flows and interface patterns while coordinating stakeholder expectations around scope and launch readiness.

Design focus

  • Structure membership journeys across access and identity moments.
  • Clarify information architecture for a global fan service.
  • Balance public UI clarity with stakeholder and launch constraints.
