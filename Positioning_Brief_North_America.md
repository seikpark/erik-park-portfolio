# Positioning Brief: North America Product Designer

작성일: 2026-05-27

## Decision

Erik Park의 포트폴리오 포지셔닝은 다음으로 확정한다.

> Product Designer with PM experience.

더 구체적인 포트폴리오 문장:

> Product Designer with PM experience, designing scalable digital products from strategy to shipped UX.

Big Tech-oriented variant:

> Product Designer with PM experience, turning complex workflows into scalable, shipped digital experiences.

한국어 기준:

> PM 경험을 바탕으로 전략부터 출시 가능한 UX까지 설계하는 Product Designer.

## Target

- Market: North America
- Primary role: Product Designer
- Secondary signals: UX/UI Designer, Product Designer - Design Systems, AI Product Designer
- Target level: Senior Product Designer 정도
- Level strategy: Lead/Manager보다 Senior IC 중심. 영어가 원어민 수준이 아닌 점은 짧고 명확한 writing, strong visuals, structured case study로 보완한다.
- Differentiator: PM/Product Owner/Design Manager 경험
- Reference bar: Microsoft, Amazon, Apple Product Designer / UX Designer postings
- Reference portfolios: Samantha Yeung for warmth/personality, Chuck Shin for systems/case study structure

## What This Should Communicate

채용자가 Erik을 볼 때 다음 순서로 이해해야 한다.

1. This person is a Product Designer.
2. He can design clear flows, interfaces, and product systems.
3. He understands product strategy and delivery because of PM experience.
4. He can work with stakeholders, developers, clients, and leadership.
5. He has shipped real products and can point to measurable outcomes.
6. He can handle ambiguity, prototype quickly, collaborate with engineering, and explain decisions clearly.
7. He communicates clearly through structured thinking, even if English is not positioned as a native-level advantage.

## What To Avoid

북미 Product Designer 지원에서는 다음 인상을 피해야 한다.

- PM으로 전환하려는 디자이너처럼 보이는 것
- 관리직/매니저 역할만 강조되어 hands-on design craft가 약해 보이는 것
- 프로젝트를 많이 했지만 깊은 case study가 부족해 보이는 것
- 브랜드명은 강하지만 실제 문제 해결 과정과 디자인 산출물이 약해 보이는 것
- "I did everything"처럼 보이고, 구체적인 design contribution이 흐려지는 것

## Messaging Rules

- Headline에서는 Product Designer를 먼저 쓴다.
- PM은 "experience" 또는 "background"로 표현한다.
- Design Manager 경험은 "design systems, documentation, team workflows"로 번역한다.
- Project Manager 경험은 "product thinking, stakeholder alignment, delivery ownership"으로 번역한다.
- 모든 case study는 final design preview를 초반에 보여준다.
- PM/운영 이야기는 디자인 결정과 결과를 강화하는 맥락으로 배치한다.
- Big Tech 지원용 case study에는 research, metrics, prototype, design system/handoff, and implementation collaboration 섹션을 반드시 넣는다.
- Samantha Yeung처럼 "In a nutshell"과 human microcopy를 활용한다.
- Chuck Shin처럼 role/team/timeline, constraints, challenge/solution, outcome proof를 명확히 보여준다.
- Senior 레벨 지원용 문장은 길고 화려한 영어보다 짧고 정확한 영어를 사용한다.
- 한 문단에는 하나의 주장만 넣고, metric/visual artifact로 증명한다.

## Recommended Case Study Order

### 1. Butler

Why:

- Strongest measurable product design impact: 40% retention increase.
- Shows UX redesign, product thinking, and delivery ownership.
- Retention rationale can be explained.

Position as:

> Product redesign that improved retention by 40%.

### 2. SM Meta-passport

Why:

- Strong brand credibility.
- Good for service design, complex user flows, and stakeholder alignment.
- UI can be public.

Position as:

> Designing a global membership service experience for entertainment fans.

### 3. Bero AI

Why:

- Microsoft, Amazon, and Apple postings repeatedly value AI, ambiguity, prototyping, and complex systems.
- Shows current product design work in an emerging AI problem space.
- Direction and feature screens can be public.
- Must be framed as in-progress because it has not launched yet.

Position as:

> Designing an AI communication experience from ambiguous problem space to product direction.

### 4. Brickmate Design Operations

Why:

- Differentiates Erik from typical Product Designer candidates.
- Shows design systems, guidelines, documentation, and scalable team workflows.
- Materials can be anonymized and shown publicly.

Position as:

> Building design systems and workflows that helped global client products ship consistently.

### Time-Limited 3-Case Set

If only three case studies can be built first:

1. Butler
2. SM Meta-passport
3. Bero AI

Then add Brickmate as the fourth case to strengthen design systems and scalability.

## Portfolio Structure Implication

Home:

- Product Designer headline
- PM background in subcopy
- UI/product visuals first
- Metrics proof bar
- Featured Work before a large capabilities section

Work:

- 4 featured case studies for Big Tech applications
- Filters start with Product Design, UX/UI, Design Systems
- Product Management filter can exist but should not lead

Case Study:

- Start with final design and impact
- Then explain problem, process, design decisions, collaboration, and outcome
- PM work appears as product thinking and delivery context

About:

- Human story is welcome, but connect it to observation, service design, collaboration, and craft
